Forest Resources

The different types of resources like Wood, Timer, bushmeat, medicines etc., provided by forests are termed Forest Resources. A forest is a dense growth of trees and other plants covering a significant amount of land

It is an ecosystem, a community of plants and animals interacting with one another and their environment. Forestry is the science involved in studying, preserving, and managing forests

    Forest's role in the environment

  1. Promoting rainfall.
  2. Reduces noise pollution.
  3. Maintains the ecological balance.
